# Objective

Implement the Conversation Intelligence layer.

This module enriches conversations with customer understanding.

The AI should learn from conversations without changing or inventing customer information.

Conversation Intelligence provides insights to the AI Runtime, Workflow Engine, CRM and business users.

---

# Scope

Implement

- Conversation Summary
- Customer Memory
- Preference Extraction
- Sentiment Analysis
- Lead Scoring
- Intent History
- AI Confidence
- Conversation Insights

No vector database.

No long-term LLM memory.

No autonomous actions.

---

# Conversation Summary

Automatically generate summaries for completed conversations.

Include

- Customer objective
- Outcome
- Actions performed
- Booking created
- Escalations
- Payment status
- Follow-up required

Store summaries with the conversation.

---

# Customer Memory

Persist reusable customer preferences.
