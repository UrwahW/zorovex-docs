# Objective

Implement the centralized Business Intelligence Platform.

This module aggregates data across every domain in Zorovex and transforms operational data into actionable business intelligence.

The BI Platform is the single source of truth for analytics, KPIs, trends, recommendations, executive dashboards, and future AI Copilot features.

No individual module should calculate business reports independently.

---

# Philosophy

Traditional software shows reports.

Zorovex explains the business.

Instead of

"You had 84 bookings."

Zorovex should answer

"Bookings increased 18% this month. Weekend demand is growing while Tuesday afternoons remain underutilized. Consider opening an additional evening slot."

The BI Platform should focus on answering business questions rather than displaying raw numbers.
