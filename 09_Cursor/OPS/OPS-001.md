# Objective

Implement the complete Business Operations module.

This module becomes the operational backbone of Zorovex.

It manages:

- Services
- Categories
- Packages
- Staff
- Working Hours
- Skills
- Rooms
- Resources
- Pricing

Future modules including AI, Bookings and Reports must consume these resources instead of creating their own copies.
