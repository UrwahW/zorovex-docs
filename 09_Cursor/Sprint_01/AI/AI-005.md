# Objective

Implement the AI Business Workflow Engine.

The AI should no longer execute isolated tools.

Instead it should execute complete business workflows.

Examples

Booking Workflow

Lead Workflow

Cancellation Workflow

Complaint Workflow

FAQ Workflow

Escalation Workflow

Service Recommendation Workflow

Upsell Workflow

---

# Philosophy

The AI behaves like a trained employee.

It understands

conversation

↓

chooses workflow

↓

executes workflow

↓

asks questions

↓

collects information

↓

calls business services

↓

completes task

The customer should never feel like they are interacting with APIs.

# AI Responsibilities

The AI should

Ask follow-up questions.

Collect missing information.

Remember previous answers.

Handle corrections.

Resume interrupted workflows.

Summarize conversations.

Never expose implementation details.
