# Objective

Implement ONLY the Location step of the Business Setup Wizard.

This step is responsible for collecting all geographical and regional information required by the CRM, AI Receptionist, Calendar, Booking Engine and future integrations.

Do not implement Working Hours.

Do not implement Services.

Do not implement Staff.

Do not implement AI configuration.

Only implement the Location step.
