# Objective

Implement the complete Services Management module.

This module becomes the single source of truth for every service offered by a business.

The AI Receptionist, Booking Engine, Staff Scheduling, Deals, Packages, Campaigns and Analytics will all use this module.

This module must be scalable for both small businesses and enterprise businesses.

---

# Responsibilities

This module manages:

- Services
- Categories
- Pricing
- Duration
- Deposits
- Visibility
- Booking Rules

It does NOT manage:

- Staff Assignment
- Packages
- Deals
- Availability
- Bookings

Those belong to later modules.

# Business Rules

Every service belongs to exactly one category.

Categories cannot be deleted while services exist.

Archived services cannot be booked.

Inactive services remain visible only to staff.

Booking Enabled determines if customers may book online.

AI Visible determines whether the AI Receptionist may recommend the service.

Featured services appear first.
