# Objective

Implement the Business Brain Management module.

This module stores all structured knowledge used by the business.

The Business Brain becomes the single source of truth for:

- AI Receptionist
- Voice Agent
- Future AI Employees
- CRM AI Features
- Recommendation Engine
- Customer Assistance

Version 1 focuses ONLY on collecting and managing Business Brain content.

Do NOT implement AI retrieval.

Do NOT implement embeddings.

Do NOT implement vector search.

Those belong to the AI Runtime.
