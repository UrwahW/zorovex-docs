# Objective

Implement ONLY the Business Availability step of the Business Setup Wizard.

This step defines when the business itself is open.

It does NOT define:

- Staff schedules
- Service availability
- Holidays
- Leaves
- Booking slots

Those belong to future modules.

---

# Why

The AI Receptionist needs to know whether the business is open before attempting bookings.

Business Availability becomes one input to the future Availability Engine.

Future Availability Engine

Business Hours

+

Staff Schedule

+

Service Rules

+

Approved Leaves

+

Holidays

+

Existing Bookings

=

Available Appointment Slots

This task implements ONLY the Business Hours layer.

# Business Rules

Business Hours represent when the business is physically available.

They do NOT guarantee appointment availability.

Appointments depend on:

Business Hours

+

Staff Availability

+

Service Availability

+

Booking Engine

Do not mix these responsibilities.
