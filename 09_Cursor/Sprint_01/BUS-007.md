# Objective

Implement the complete Staff Management module.

This module manages all people who provide services inside the business.

Staff are separate from CRM Users.

A staff member may or may not have CRM login access.

Examples

Receptionist

Hair Stylist

Doctor

Dentist

Therapist

Trainer

Teacher

Mechanic

Manager

Assistant

The AI Receptionist and Booking Engine will use this module to determine appointment availability.

# Business Rules

A staff member can perform many services.

A service can belong to many staff members.

Staff schedules override business opening hours.

If a staff member is unavailable, the Booking Engine must not offer appointments.

Staff can exist without CRM accounts.

CRM accounts can later be linked.
