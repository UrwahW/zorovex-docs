Transform the AI Runtime from a prompt-driven chatbot into a deterministic Business AI.
The LLM should generate natural language—not make business decisions.
Business logic, workflows, policies, pricing, and permissions must be resolved by the platform before any LLM is called.

Intent Classification
Before any provider is called, classify every incoming message.
Supported intents include:
Booking
Reschedule
Cancel
Pricing
Services
Availability
Staff
Business Information
Promotions
Complaint
Payment
General FAQ
Human Assistance
Small Talk
Unsupported / Off-topic
Classification should be deterministic and reusable across all channels.
Topic Guardrails
Prevent the AI from acting as a general-purpose chatbot.
Examples:
Allowed:
"How much is a bridal package?"
"Do you have appointments tomorrow?"
"Can I cancel my booking?"
Rejected:
"Explain quantum physics."
"Write Python code."
"Who won the World Cup?"
Homework or unrelated requests.
The AI should politely redirect users back to supported business topics.
Goal Resolution Engine
Resolve goals before calling the LLM.
Examples:
Book Appointment
Modify Booking
Cancel Booking
Collect Lead
Answer Business Question
Escalate to Human
Request More Information
The LLM must never invent goals.

Knowledge Retrieval Layer

Workflow Router
Before using the LLM:
Determine whether a deterministic workflow exists.
Examples:
Appointment Booking
Cancellation
Lead Capture
Payment Reminder
Human Escalation
If a workflow can fully satisfy the request, bypass the LLM.
Tool Execution Layer
Business actions must execute through registered tools.
Examples:
Create Booking
Cancel Booking
Update Customer
Send Payment Link
Assign Staff
Create CRM Task
The LLM may request actions but cannot execute them directly.
Response Validation
Every generated response must pass validation before delivery.
Validate:
Pricing accuracy
Policy compliance
Business rule compliance
Hallucinations against retrieved facts
Unsupported service claims
Required disclaimers
Escalation triggers
Invalid responses should be regenerated or replaced with deterministic fallbacks.
