# Objective

Transform the Platform Console into the operational control center for Zorovex.

Platform Administrators must be able to create, provision, manage, support and impersonate customer organizations without using the database or Rails console.

The Platform Console is an internal product.

It must have a completely different experience from the customer Business Workspace.

---

# Philosophy

Business users operate their company.

Platform users operate Zorovex.

These are two different products.

The Platform Console should feel like Stripe Dashboard, Clerk, Auth0 and Vercel Team Management combined.

Platform users should never see the customer dashboard as their primary interface.
