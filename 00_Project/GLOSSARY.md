# Zorovex Glossary

---

## Platform

The SaaS application operated by Zorovex.

Contains every organization.

---

## Organization

A tenant.

Owns all business data.

---

## Workspace

The CRM used by one organization.

---

## Platform Console

Administrative interface for Zorovex staff.

---

## Business Catalog

Canonical commercial information.

Contains

Services

Packages

Promotions

Pricing Rules

Holiday Rules

Categories

---

## Business Knowledge

Conversational information.

Examples

FAQs

Policies

Notes

Business descriptions

Staff biographies

---

## Business Readiness

Computed operational readiness.

Determines whether the organization is capable of running
its AI receptionist.

---

## AI Runtime

Deterministic orchestration engine.

Coordinates

Intent

Knowledge

Rules

Workflows

Providers

Validation

Confidence

---

## Provider

Language generation implementation.

Examples

Mock

OpenAI

Anthropic

Gemini

---

## Workflow

Deterministic business automation.

Examples

Booking

Cancellation

Lead Capture

Complaint

---

## Guardrail

A deterministic rule that prevents unsafe responses.

---

## Tool

A callable backend capability.

Example

Create Booking

Create Customer

Send Email

---

## Capability

A business function evaluated by Business Readiness.

---

## AI Studio

Owner interface for configuring AI.

---

## Runtime Inspector

Engineering interface showing every AI decision.

---

## Conversation

A customer communication thread.

---

## Contact

CRM representation of a customer.

---

## Thread Ownership

AI

Human

Hybrid

---

## Channel

Communication source.

WhatsApp

Instagram

Facebook

Voice

Website

Email

---

## Simulation

Temporary data created by Playground.

Never becomes production CRM data.

---

## Knowledge Source

Structured information provided to the AI.

---

## Platform Event

System-wide domain event.

---

## Domain Event

Business event inside an organization.

---

## Confidence

Runtime confidence score.

Never an LLM confidence.

Always deterministic.
